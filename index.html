rp calculator
this calculates the gold your rp log will earn.
The current rate is 100 gold per 1000 words, divided by the number of participants. The returned gold will be a factor of 25, rounded down. input numbers only!
The number of people in the roleplay. Each should have contributed equally. The total word count of the roleplay log. It should be 1000 words or more.

fp calculator
this calculates how much FP your submission will earn. The total calculated is for a single relationship.
hint: you can't stack multiple bonuses for FP.
Interactions in the group setting and group official AUs (dream events) are considered 'canon'. Collaborative interactions are when users have contributed equal work to an image submission. You can count a roleplay log for FP if it's logged and the word count is 1000 or more (total between users). Heart events are special events that can be done once the FP between two characters has reached a certain amount. Birthday bonuses are for celebrating a character's birthday! They can be submitted any time during the season of the character's birthday. To get FP for items, you must give the item to the other character during a roleplay (min 1000 words). input numbers only! Leave blank if none. Event bonuses are given out during group events (mini-events, festival events, dream events). Specified by the event journal. input numbers only! Leave blank if none. This is for special conditions (ie. fp boosting item).
